SpeakUP AI — Debate Training Structure
=======================================

LEVEL 1 — Foundations
----------------------
Who it's for: Complete beginners, students new to structured debate
Goal: Understand how debate works and deliver a basic argument confidently

Topics covered:
  1. What is debate? (types: Policy, Lincoln-Douglas, Public Forum, Parliamentary)
  2. The anatomy of an argument — Claim, Warrant, Impact (CWI model)
  3. Building your first case (affirmative vs. negative)
  4. How to flow (taking notes during a round)
  5. Cross-examination basics — what to ask, what to avoid
  6. Writing and delivering a rebuttal
  7. Speaking order, timing, and structure of a round
  8. Practice: Deliver a 2-minute constructive speech

AI Coach focus:
  - Encouraging and simple language
  - Step-by-step explanations
  - Immediate feedback on argument structure
  - Suggests improvements without overwhelming

Route:  /debate/level-1
API:    /api/debate/level-1


LEVEL 2 — Strategy                                    ← NEXT TO BUILD
----------------------
Who it's for: Students who understand basics and want to win rounds
Goal: Think strategically, adapt to opponents, and run complex arguments

Topics covered:
  1. Turns — how to flip an opponent's argument against them
  2. Defensive vs. offensive strategy
  3. Reading the flow — what's winning and what's not
  4. Advanced cross-examination (trapping opponents, setting up arguments)
  5. Block files — preparing responses before the round
  6. Time allocation — where to spend your speaking time
  7. Running multiple off-case positions
  8. Practice: Run a full cross-ex exchange

AI Coach focus:
  - Strategic thinking prompts
  - Opponent simulation
  - Flow analysis and advice
  - Push back on weak arguments

Route:  /debate/level-2
API:    /api/debate/level-2


LEVEL 3 — Competition
----------------------
Who it's for: Experienced debaters preparing for tournaments
Goal: Perform under pressure, adapt to any judge, and win elimination rounds

Topics covered:
  1. Tournament structure (prelims, elims, brackets)
  2. Judge adaptation — reading paradigms, adjusting style
  3. Theory and procedural arguments
  4. Kritik debate (K debate) basics
  5. Speed and clarity — when to spread, when to slow down
  6. Elimination round pressure management
  7. Post-round analysis — what to fix before the next round
  8. Practice: Full timed round simulation

AI Coach focus:
  - High-level, technical coaching
  - Brutally honest feedback
  - Tournament-specific prep
  - Mindset and confidence coaching

Route:  /debate/level-3
API:    /api/debate/level-3


SHARED API PATTERN
------------------
Each level has its own API route that:
  - Takes: topic, argument, user's speech text, experience level
  - Returns: structured feedback, suggested improvements, example arguments
  - Model: claude-opus-4-8
  - Stored in: /src/app/api/debate/level-[n]/route.ts
  - Component: /src/components/debate/Level[n].tsx
  - Page wrapper: /src/app/debate/level-[n]/page.tsx
